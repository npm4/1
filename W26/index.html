PICT B.E. Course Structure

First Year (FE)

  • Engineering Mathematics I

    Fundamentals of calculus, matrices, and complex numbers.

  • Basic Electrical Engineering

    Basic concepts of electrical circuits and machines.

  • Engineering Physics

    Principles of mechanics, optics, and semiconductors.

Second Year (SE)

  • Data Structures

    Introduction to arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, trees, and graphs.

  • Digital Electronics

    Combinational and sequential logic circuits.

  • Object-Oriented Programming

    Concepts of classes, objects, inheritance, and polymorphism in C++/Java.

Third Year (TE)

  • Operating Systems

    Processes, threads, scheduling, memory and file management.

  • Database Management Systems

    Relational models, SQL, transactions and normalization.

  • Computer Networks

    Network layers, IP addressing, routing, and protocols.

Final Year (BE)

  • Machine Learning

    Supervised and unsupervised learning techniques, model evaluation.

  • Cloud Computing

    Virtualization, IaaS, PaaS, SaaS models, and cloud architecture.

  • Project Phase I & II

    Capstone project development in teams with real-world problems.
